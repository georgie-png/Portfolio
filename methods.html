Crip Disobedient Methods?

The methodology for this project is a Disobedient Action Research Methodology. As the above texture and indeterminate diagram tries to highlight, this methodology disobediently refuses to be generalized or normalized into set terms of engagement. Instead this projects methodology aims to make wiggle room for bodyminds to access, be in touch with and shape the sociotechnical infrastructures and matters they are impacted by.

To build up my projects Disobedient Action Research methodology I crip the methods of TiTIPi to ask what room can collective embodied inquiries make within infrastructuring. With this methodology disobediently being intimate with and building up collective expertise around what is and can be within reach socially and technically through independent action. In this Disobedient methodology it also aims to re-frame what actions can be taken within academic research.

In this project this crip trans*methodology crossed from collective practice, terminal consoles, bank interfaces, parties, workshops, messaging stickers, VPN's and soft cushions. Coalescing together these many surfaces and tables of research it aims to question what collaborative actions can unfold more affirmative infrastructure, both social and technical.
