Disobedient Methods?

The methodology for this project is a Disobedient Action Research Methodology. As the above texture and indeterminate diagram tries to highlight, this methodology disobediently refuses to be generalized or normalized into set operations. Instead this projects methodology aims to make wiggle room for bodyminds to access, be in touch with and shape the sociotechnical infrastructures and matters they are impacted by and misfit within.

To build up my projects Disobedient Action Research methodology I crip the methods of TiTIPi to ask what room can collective embodied inquiries make within infrastructuring. With this methodology making room to be intimate with and build up collective expertise to question what affirmative infrastructures, practices and relations are within reach socially and technically through independent action. Through this disobedient methodology it also aims to re-frame what actions can be taken within academic research, moving to ask what lives are liveable beyond assimilation norms of access on institutional terms.

Through my trans-methodological approach, it crosses from collective practices, debugging, terminal consoles, bank interfaces, parties, workshops, messaging stickers, VPN's and soft cushions. Coalescing together these many surfaces, textures and tables of research I aims to make sense of what collaborative practices can make room for communities to manifest more affirmative infrastructures.

Protocols

These protocols offer up some of the ways these methods have loosely unfolded throughout this research. These protocols themselves are loose mappings of the what have happened in the context of this research and aim to highlight how these methods are meant to be improvised, situated and felt out in action.

Processing Misfitting

This protocol maps the ways in which collaborators and I made sense of our experiences of misfitting within configurations, their infrastructures and systems. This process is made up of collective action research and reflection aims to form a distributed set of expertise that can start to understand how these systems affect us, and how we have hacked, countered and disoriented their norms in response.

Frictious Infrastructures

This is similar to the Processing Misfitting but it focuses on making of infrastructures and cripping of practices to imagine how collaborators and I could be manifest our affirming infrastructures now. The frictious infrastructures range from accessible websites and technical docs, to poetic maintenance jobs and disobedient access requests.
