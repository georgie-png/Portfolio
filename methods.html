Disobedient Methods?

The methodology for this project is a Disobedient Action Research Methodology. As the above texture and indeterminate diagram tries to highlight, this methodology disobediently refuses to be generalized or normalized into set operations. Instead this projects methodology aims to make wiggle room for bodyminds to access, be in touch with and shape the sociotechnical infrastructures and matters they are impacted by and misfit within.

To build up my projects Disobedient Action Research methodology I crip the methods of TiTIPi to ask what room can collective embodied inquiries make within infrastructuring. With this methodology making room to be intimate with and build up collective expertise to question what affirmative infrastructures, practices and relations are within reach socially and technically through independent action. Through this disobedient methodology it also aims to re-frame what actions can be taken within academic research, moving to ask what lives are liveable beyond assimilation norms of access on institutional terms.

Through my trans-methodological approach, it crosses from collective practices, debugging, terminal consoles, bank interfaces, parties, workshops, messaging stickers, VPN's and soft cushions. Coalescing together these many surfaces, textures and tables of research I aims to make sense of what collaborative practices can make room for communities to manifest more affirmative infrastructures.

Protocols

These protocols offer up some of the ways these methods have unfolded throughout this research. These protocols themselves are loose mappings of what has happened in the context of this research and aim to highlight how these methods are meant to be improvised, situated, figured out and shaped by action.

Practicing Protocols

Practicing Protocols shares how these methods unfolded into a set of accessible workshops that aimed to make wiggle room for people to come into contact with and reshape network infrastructures with their embodied expertise and for their situated background and community.

This protocol unfolded at both the Collective Infrastructures combined panel, as at the Serving In-grid workshop to form collective expertise of and counter figures, relations and imaginaries to the ones we have inherited from configurations.

This mapping above gives shape to how this protocol took action, moving from accessible technical documentation to feel out the embodied misfitting within these infrastructures, to then make sense of them through our own situated figures and terms.

Improving FLOSS Protocols

Improvising Floss Protocols shares how these methods unfolded around making access with FLOSS technologies throughout this research. This protocol shares how collaborators and I wiggled room between the misfitting of FLOSS and our access knowledges to improvise access technologies, as well as make sense of the limits of access within FLOSS right now.

This protocol unfolded from the the Configure-Able Infrastructures Workshop I organised with NEoN digital, making the Cozy-ish and Cozy Night and Day themes, as well as manifesting the Serving Cozy install. Within each of these this protocol made room for collaborators and I to wiggle room within the technologies we were practicing for them to be shaped by and affirm our access needs and desires.

This mapping above gives shape to how this protocol took action, making sense of how FLOSS technologies we had in reach misfitted with the access knowledges of the group. From this misfitting moving to wiggle room, improvise and shape the imaginaries and capacities of FLOSS through collective practices of access making.
