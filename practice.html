Practice

This page shares the practice, inquiries and research this project took towards manifesting Configure-able Infrastructures informed through critical access and crip practices. These different Cripped Tables of inquiries emerged through my Disobedient Action Research Methods and are split into two sections, called Inquiries and Background. These aim to offer up together the foreground of the main inquiries as well as the background to this research that they are contoured from.

Inquiries

This section shares the main inquiries, practice and collaborative research into manifesting Configure-able Infrastructures in action. These inquiries take on the form of workshops, focus groups, technical docs, speculative infrastructures and * * * * *. These are split into three sections which each ask their own unique questions around how critical access and crip theory can disorient the normalising relations of configurations to instead shape infrastructures around the bodies they impact.

Multi column

Configure-Able Infrastructures


These inquiries offer up how critical access knowledge disobediently makes Wiggle Room for the prescribed configurations of infrastructures to be disoriented, hacked and reconfigured through the expertise and access knowledges of the bodies they impact.

In-Grid's In-frastructures


These inquiries offer up how critical access can be brought into collaborations within non-disability communities, not as extra time, but as a way to disobediently Wiggle Room within infrastructures to make accessible practices of interdependent sense making and direct action.

Cozy-Cloud


These inquiries share how the Cozy-Cloud has disobediently made Wiggle Room to crip server and network infrastructures through unfolding practices of access making and life affirmation.

Background

This section shares more of the background to the research. This is predominantly to make known what this work is in retreat from, but also what research practices have shaped these inquiries. These reflections are here to disobediently situate this work and make Wiggle Room to balance what is in focus, with what it has unfolded from.

Background to Retreat


These background inquiries share the sense making of configurations of automation, AI and decision making. Here disobediently making Wiggle Room to share what this research is turning away from.

Research Practice


This section shares the research practices that have made room for these inquiries to unfold. In it I bring focus to a workshop I ran with Batool Desouky called Making Sense With Obsidian, in which we shared our approach to gathering and bringing together our research and inquiries.
