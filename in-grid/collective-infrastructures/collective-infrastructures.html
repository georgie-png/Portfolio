Collective Infrastructures

This was a combined panel run by In-grid members at 4S/EASST 2024 in Amsterdamn. For this we brought together a panel of different collectives and communities who are actively practising and re-imagining what infrastructures are through situated practice and direct action. This included members of TITiPI, NEoN Digital, Júlia Nueno Guitart, and SHAPE. Along side the panel In-grid members also ran the first iteration of the Practicing Protocols workshop which made room for collaborators to get in touch with network infrastructure, and to make sense of their configurations, protocols and relations from their own expertise and knowledges.

This combined panel and workshop offered up the many ways that collective practices and contexts manifest and situate infrastructural practice with and for different politics, expertise and capacities. In this approach aiming to share how infrastructures are shaped by the communities and desires that manifest them. With the workshop especially bringing focus to the ways this can be done within technical practices by making room for peoples backgrounds and embodied expertise to come into contact with and shape how these infrastructures are encoded and manifested through situated practices.
