En-crip-ing Time

This entry shares En-crip-ing time, which is a crip infrastructure made in collaboration with Mariana Marangoni. With En-crip-ing time we made room for crip time in network time, now and in the future. Through this we questioned the intimacies and care needed to not only manifest, but maintain crip infrastructures like these. This work was presented at Mesh Festival, as well as an essay about it published with Brand-New-Life Journal.

The En-crip-ing time infrastructure is run on the Cozy-Cloud server as a way to crip the internal clock and background of the server. It does this by poetically cripping the cron jobs (deterministic maintenance jobs) of the server to be chronic jobs (indeterminate, indefinite and intimate care). In this creative inquiry making room for Mariana and I to make sense of our misfitting within the configurations of network time, to then imagine how we want to poetically hack it now to imagine crips beyond its limits.
