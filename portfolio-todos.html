Feedback

Initial website thoughts: I like the look of the site, no notes really there.

  1. Home page starts a bit abruptly, possibly have a more general landing page so i know what I'm looking at - the about sentence does a good job of that so maybe that should be there - otherwise I wasn't clear that Configure-able Infrastructures was the name of the project, who you are etc.

  2. In general some of the sections could use more of a grounding intro - for example, the In-grid's infrastructures section button should say In-grid is a collective. You might need to be a bit more didactic in general. i.e. have the longer text about what the inquires are on the page itself, and the button/link just says Inquires into In-grid collective's infrastructures.

  3. More images particularly of the creative practice aspects.

  4. I almost think your practice and methods pages could be one, all practice. I think the methods could be described as how-tos i.e. remetaphoring/practicing protocols, doc writing etc. If I understand the feedback correctly, they want more more of a summary of how you might replicate the methods? I may have misunderstood.

  5. Sub menus would be useful, as you have a lot of nested parts I found it a little tricky to navigate at times.
